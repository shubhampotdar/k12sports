The Indian education system offers one of the most important opportunities to improve the quality of sport and physical activity for all Indians. From daycares to universities, the health of the nation and the long-term success of our athletes can be promoted through sport and activity programming at every level of education.Physical inactivity is a serious problem among our Indian children. It’s leading to dramatic increases in childhood obesity and significantly increased long-term risks for related illnesses and symptoms such as type 2 diabetes, cancer, stroke and heart disease. Physical inactivity also contributes to poor cognitive development, low academic achievement, poor socialisation, and lessened development of community.
